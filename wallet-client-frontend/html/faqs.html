Frequently Asked Questions

What is a digital wallet?

A digital wallet is an online service that allows you to store and manage funds electronically for making transactions.

Is my money safe in the digital wallet?

Yes, digital wallets use encryption and secure authentication methods to keep your funds safe.

How do I add money to my digital wallet?

You can add money using bank transfers, credit/debit cards, or linked accounts.

Can I send money to other users?

Yes, you can transfer money instantly to other users within the digital wallet system.
